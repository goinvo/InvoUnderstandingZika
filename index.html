Understanding Zika

USDA & Felipe Dana/AP

What is Zika

Zika is a virus spreading quickly throughout Latin America that is carried by mosquitoes and causes mild illness. There has been an unconfirmed link between Zika-infected pregnant women and a condition called microcephaly, in which babies are born with underdeveloped brains.
